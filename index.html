About Us

John Allen Motorcycle Parts Store is a local shop in Pillar Village known for high-quality motorcycle parts, accessories, and reliable repair services. Previously, appointment scheduling relied on manual methods, requiring customers to walk in or contact the shop directly.

Our mission is to provide trusted service and premium motorcycle parts to every rider in our community. With trained mechanics, reliable workmanship, and honest service, we ensure convenience, transparency, and value in every visit.

Our Services

Engine Repair
Process of diagnosing, servicing , and fixing the engine to restore it to proper working order.
Tire Replacement
The act of installing new tires to change worn out tires.
Oil Change
Draining the old oil, replacing the old filter, and refilling the engine with fresh oil.
Brake Service
A comprehesive maintainance procedure that involves a through inspection and service of the entire braking system to ensure it functions safety and effectively.
